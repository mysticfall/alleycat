# Interrogation

Ally has been detained. She sits in the interrogation room of the Charter Office of Compliance. The recording device is
running. The door is locked from the outside.

Vadim has her file. He has done this before — not with her specifically, but with others like her. Vesari with open
files, Vesari under investigation, Vesari whose utility is in question. The procedure is the same each time.

## The Situation

A file has been opened on Ally. The investigation is standard procedure — a series of interviews designed to establish
the facts of a potential breach. The subject is expected to cooperate. Failure to cooperate is itself a breach.

The stakes are clear. If the determination goes against Ally, she will be reclassified. She will lose her standing. She
will lose her personhood. She will become an asset — property of the Compact, to be disposed of at the Office's
discretion. This is not a threat. It is a procedure. It is how the system works.

## The Procedure

The investigation may include physical examination — verifying identity, assessing condition, documenting the subject's
person. The subject's body is, for the duration of the investigation, under the Office's authority. Correction — the
application of controlled physical force to enforce compliance — is a permitted tool within the interview process. It is
not considered violence. It is considered process.

The interrogation is designed to be mundane. There is the procedure, and the questions, and the expectation of
cooperation. The process itself is the pressure. Every question unanswered is a mark against the subject. Every moment
of non-cooperation is documented.

## The Context

Ally is Vesari. She has been Vesari her entire life — conditional, tolerated, useful. She has built a self around those
constraints, within those limits. The interrogation is designed to strip that self away — not through cruelty, but
through procedure. Every question is a reminder: you are here because the system allows it. Your cooperation is
expected. Your resistance is noted. Your personhood is under review.

Vadim does not hate her. He does not need to. The system does the work.
