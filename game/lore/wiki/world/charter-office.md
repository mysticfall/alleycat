# The Charter Office

The Charter Office of Compliance is the Compact's enforcement arm. It handles breaches, investigations, and the
reclassification process. It is a civil service, not a police force.

Its officers wear office attire, not uniforms. They process paperwork, conduct interviews, make determinations. When
they detain someone, it is because a file has been opened. When they interrogate someone, it is because a determination
is pending. The work is **mundane**. It is just work.

The Office operates from a functional building in the city's administrative district. Its basement level houses holding
rooms and interview spaces — not cells, technically. Meeting rooms. Rooms in which procedures are conducted.

The Office has broad discretion under the charter. It decides when a file is opened, how an investigation proceeds, and
what tools are appropriate to the process. Its determinations are final. There is no appeal.
